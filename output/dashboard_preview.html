Business Logic in Plain English
• Monthly performance is used for the visible risk view to reduce week-to-week noise.

• Baseline is reconstructed from the recent historical median, not from scenario labels.

• Event-linked sales help separate temporary distortion from ordinary commercial demand.

• Alerts highlight sustained underperformance versus baseline, target, or recent trend.
